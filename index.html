Our Projects

Across Continents

Heritage Structures – Structural Investigation

India and New Zealand

Our team led an extensive review of structural drawings and on-site non-destructive testing of existing RC buildings to understand typical construction practices and highlight potential seismic vulnerabilities. We developed a detailed seismic assessment methodology and demonstrated its successful application across multiple projects. Our team recommends appropriate repair and retrofitting methodologies based on these detailed technical investigations.

Residential, Commercial and Industrial Buildings

India

Our team delivered high-quality, Vastu-compliant 2D floor plans and realistic 3D renderings for various projects across India. We produced precise structural designs from initial concept to construction-ready blueprints while managing all design revisions and providing dedicated on-site investigation during the construction phase. Beyond design, our team prepared detailed project estimations and handled contract management while liaising with architects, owners, and subcontractors. We provided comprehensive rate analysis, ensured project profitability and quality compliance via regular site inspections, and implemented cost-saving strategies through resource and material optimization. Our team collaborated closely with project partners to ensure on-time delivery according to technical specifications.

Wastewater Tunnel Project and Underground Retaining Structures

Auckland, NZ

The Central Interceptor is New Zealand's largest wastewater tunnel project, consisting of a 14.7 km main tunnel, multiple shafts, a major pump station, and substantial wastewater network infrastructure. Our team contributed to various phases of design, such as preliminary design (PD) and detailed design (DD), addressed design change requests (DCR), and assisted with construction phase support (CPS). We were involved in the modelling, analysis, and design of temporary works for control chambers and pump stations, temporary and permanent works for shaft lining and manhole structures, and the design of box culverts and retaining structures. Our team also drove end-to-end procurement, commercial leadership, financial management, cost-optimization, schedule management, and complex claims negotiation.

Airport Domestic Terminal

Auckland, NZ

A brand-new domestic terminal, integrated into the current international terminal, is a key feature of the programme, delivering the gateway Auckland and New Zealand need. Our team refined project delivery and commercial performance by managing end-to-end claims, forecasting, and schedule management. Additionally, our team led a digital transformation using AI-driven tools like Power BI to automate reporting and provide real-time financial insights.

Retaining Wall Structure

NSW, Australia

This project involved slope remediation design for two retaining walls along a mountainous road in NSW after retained slopes along Jenolan Caves Road at Hampton failed during heavy rainfall. Our team was involved in the design and internal peer review of the piled barrier retaining wall structure.

Footbridge and Vehicular Bridge

Hong Kong

This project consists of a footbridge connecting Amoy Plaza and the East Kowloon Cultural Centre (EKCC) across Ngau Tau Kok Road and Highway Flyover no. K56. The Amoy Plaza footbridge structure consists of a steel truss bridge. Additionally, the redevelopment of Siu Ho Depot provides infrastructure and enabling works for future residential and public facilities. Our team was involved in the design of steel structures and steel connections for five link bridges (footbridges). In addition, our team handled the modelling and analysis of the carriage bridge at Tai Ho Interchange and prepared the necessary design calculations and reports.

Bridge Structural Assessment

Scotland

The Naver Bridge is an 80+ year-old structure that required a review of traffic load models for UK highway structures, considering heavier vehicles (SV80, ALL 40T, and Piling Rig loads). Our team was involved in modelling and analysing these additional bridge loads and assessing the capacity of the bridge's structural elements to handle increased loading.
